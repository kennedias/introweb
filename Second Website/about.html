About us

"We believe in art in any stage"



Drawings Store is about a group of independent artists from some cities in the world. They decided to give a opportunity for people to show and sells your drawings, even for no professional one. Bob is leading the project that is based in one experience he had passed. He as experienced designer was needing some inspiration and could got exactly he was looking for in a 7 years old child drawing.


He was in a cafe and the child was in a side table of him. This fact bring him an idea that for some different reasons a lot of people time to time are looking for a drawing.


So the Drawings Store is about it: a place to put in touch people that need a drawing and people that do drawings no mather a level of experience.


Take a paper and a pencil or a pen and start now!
